Lebnah

Strained yogurt, Greek yogurt,[2] yogurt cheese, sack yogurt or kerned yogurt is yogurt that has been strained to remove most of its whey, resulting in a thicker consistency than normal unstrained yogurt, while still preserving the distinctive sour taste of yogurt.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Desserts

instructions

step 1

Line a fine-mesh strainer with cheesecloth or a coffee filter. Set it over a bowl in the refrigerator.

step 2

Pour the yogurt into the lined strainer. Fold the cheesecloth or coffee filter over the yogurt to cover it.

step 3

Let the yogurt drain for at least 8 hours, or overnight, until it reaches the desired consistency. Thicker labneh will take longer to drain.

step 4

Once drained, transfer the labneh to a serving bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and garnish with fresh mint.

step 5

Serve labneh with pita bread or vegetables for dipping.

ingredients

1 liter (4 cups) whole milk yogurt

Olive oil, for serving

Fresh mint, for serving

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
