Knafeh

Knafeh[4] (Arabic: كنافة) is a traditional Arab dessert, made with spun pastry called kataifi,[5][6][7] soaked in a sweet, sugar-based syrup called attar, and typically layered with cheese, or with other ingredients such as clotted cream, pistachio or nuts, depending on the region.[

prep time

20 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

step 2

Break the phyllo dough into 1-2 inch pieces. You can pulse it a few times in a food processor to help with this step.

step 3

In a large bowl, toss the phyllo dough with the melted butter until well coated.

step 4

Press half of the dough mixture into the bottom of a greased baking pan, forming a thin layer.

step 5

(Optional) If using feta, drain the feta after soaking and crumble it with the mozzarella cheese in a food processor until it resembles a coarse meal. If using goat cheese, simply crumble it.

step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

step 2

Break the phyllo dough into 1-2 inch pieces. You can pulse it a few times in a food processor to help with this step.

step 3

In a large bowl, toss the phyllo dough with the melted butter until well coated.

step 4

Press half of the dough mixture into the bottom of a greased baking pan, forming a thin layer.

step 5

(Optional) If using feta, drain the feta after soaking and crumble it with the mozzarella cheese in a food processor until it resembles a coarse meal. If using goat cheese, simply crumble it.

step 6

Spread the cheese mixture over the dough layer in the pan.

step 7

Break the phyllo dough into 1-2 inch pieces. You can pulse it a few times in a food processor to help with this step.

step 8

Bake for 20-30 minutes, or until the kanafeh is golden brown and crispy on the edges and bottom.

step 9

Let cool slightly before serving warm. Drizzle with simple syrup or top with apple and/or pear sauce.

step 10

(Optional) Garnish with crushed pistachios before serving.

ingredients

1/2 pound shredded phyllo dough (kataifi dough, kunefe dough, or kunafa dough)

1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded

2 cups feta cheese, crumbled (soaked in water for 1 hour) OR goat cheese, crumbled

1/2 cup simple syrup OR 1/4 cup apple and/or pear sauce

(Optional) Crushed pistachios for garnish
