Chicken fayitas

These easy chicken fajitas are a classic combination of seasoned chicken strips, onions, and peppers. The homemade fajita seasoning adds a punch of not-too-spicy flavor, and dinner is ready in about 20 minutes.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Main course

instructions

step 1

In a small bowl, combine the chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix to blend thoroughly.

step 2

Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips across the grain. Coat the chicken breast strips thoroughly with all of the seasoning mixture.

step 3

Heat the vegetable oil in a large, heavy skillet over medium-high heat.

step 4

Add the spice-coated chicken to the hot skillet. Saut√© until cooked through and opaque, about 5 minutes.

step 5

Remove the chicken to a plate and set aside. Add the sliced onions and pepper strips to the skillet . Cook the vegetables, stirring frequently, for about 5 minutes, or until just tender.

step 6

Add the chicken strips back to the skillet, toss, and heat through.

step 6

Add the chicken strips back to the skillet, toss, and heat through.

step 7

Drizzle the fajitas with the lime juice and serve with warm tortillas and garnishes.

ingredients

2 teaspoons chili powder

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 teaspoon paprika

3/4 teaspoon garlic powder

1/2 teaspoon onion powder

1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 1/2 pounds boneless chicken breasts

1 large onion, thinly sliced

3 medium bell peppers, multi-colored, sliced into thin strips

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

2 tablespoons lime juice

6 to 12 (6-inch) flour tortillas, warm, for serving

Guacamole, sour cream, cheese, salsa, cilantro, or lime wedges, optional, for garnish

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons
